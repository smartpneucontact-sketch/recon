SMS Notifications — Program Information

Last updated: May 2026

Atlantic Subaru Recon is an internal vehicle reconditioning workflow application used by Atlantic Subaru staff (managers, sales associates, service advisors, and recon team members). This page describes the SMS notification program available to those staff members.

What the program does

The application sends short, transactional text messages to opted-in dealership employees when:

  • A vehicle reconditioning job is flagged urgent by a manager, sales associate, or service advisor.
  • A reconditioning job is marked complete by the recon team.

Messages are operational and time-sensitive. They are not marketing and are not sent to customers — only to authenticated dealership staff who have opted in.

How users opt in

Opt-in is collected inside the authenticated employee web application. Each user has a Receive SMS alerts switch on their profile in the Users management screen. When the switch is enabled, the user (or the manager acting on the user's verbal or written consent) sees and agrees to the following on-screen text:

By turning this on, the user consents to receive operational text messages from Atlantic Subaru Recon — only when a job is flagged urgent or marked done. Message and data rates may apply. Frequency varies. Reply STOP to unsubscribe at any time.

The application records the timestamp at which each user's opt-in was captured. Opting out clears that record.

Sample messages

Atlantic Subaru Recon: URGENT — P12345 (delivery bay 120) flagged by Sally Sales. Reply STOP to unsubscribe.
Atlantic Subaru Recon: DONE — P12345 (delivery bay 120) finished by Rico Recon. Reply STOP to unsubscribe.

Message frequency

Variable, depending on dealership activity. Typical volume is 1–10 messages per opted-in employee per day. Some days will have no messages at all.

Cost

Message and data rates may apply, charged by the recipient's mobile carrier. Atlantic Subaru does not charge employees for receiving these messages.

How to stop receiving messages

  • Reply STOP to any message to unsubscribe immediately. Other keywords that work: CANCEL, END, QUIT, UNSUBSCRIBE.
  • Reply HELP to receive help / contact information.
  • Or, ask your manager to toggle off the Receive SMS alerts switch on your user profile in the application.

Privacy

Phone numbers and opt-in state are stored only inside the Atlantic Subaru Recon application's private database. They are not sold, rented, or shared with any third party except the SMS delivery provider (Twilio Inc.) for the purpose of delivering the messages described above.
